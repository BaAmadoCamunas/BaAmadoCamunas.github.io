Reel showcasing a variety of rigs for games and animation, including a realistic biped with FK/IK limbs, stretch and squash, facial bone-based controls and prop interaction; an animation-oriented biped with expressive facial controls and hybrid FK/IK skirt; a quadruped with FK/IK, follows, non-roll and stretch/squash; plus a bow and a vehicle rig using IK handles, constraints and Set Driven Keys.

Featured Work

Biped Rig - Nyra

Real-time character rig with FK/IK limbs and spine, featuring stretch and squash systems optimized for game performance. Facial setup includes eye and lip follow systems; clothing uses Proximity Pin, Motion Paths and a hybrid FK/IK skirt for secondary motion.

Quadruped Rig - Fox

Quadruped rig with FK/IK systems for spine, legs and tail. Includes bone-based facial rig with primary and secondary controls, plus FK/IK ears for expressive animation. Stretch and squash systems on limbs, spine and tail provide enhanced control for dynamic poses.

Rigging Tool - Rig Control Color & Render Manager

Rigging tool that allows to standardize color control workflows and automate the configuration of Arnold curve rendering. It helps assign a color to controls (customized or through the index) and render controls.
