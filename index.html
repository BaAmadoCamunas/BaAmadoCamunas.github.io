Recent projects



Qve App

Mobile application created for the Community of Madrid which aims to help rural women who suffer gender violence through information, a test for their diagnosis that specifies the type of gender violence that they suffer, and self esteem excercises and relaxation audios to help them on their daily basis.



Developed with Flutter



Know more

4 different sections

The application has 4 different sections and a contact screen. In the informative section, users can find documentation about gender violence and its types.

Diagnosis and self help

This application also includes a test that identifies the type of violence suffered by the user by answering some questions. It also includes some exercises and audios to promote self esteem.

















A Flower's Dream: The Beginning

2D Dating Sim with mechanics of administration of a flower shop. The video game focuses on the history and evolution of the protagonist's relationship with the different characters that pass through his/her store.



Developed with Unity



Know more

Visual novel

The main mechanic of this phase consists on reading the dialogues between the characters and the choice of answers on which the future of the adventure will depend.

Flower's minigame

The main mechanic of this minigame consists on selecting 5 non-repeating flowers to form a bouquet according to what the client requests and increase or decrease the affinity.

















Tiny Tussle

Tiny Tussle is a shooting game on which players must collect gems to win.



Developed with Phaser.io



Know more

Different stages

At the beginning of the game each player will have to choose a character. Then, they can select which of the 3 stages they want to play. Each user will start at a different point on the map and both must try to reach the gems as soon as possible.

Characters with special abilities and random weapons

Players will be able to make use of the unique ability of each character and the weapons that appear randomly on the stage to win their opponents.
