About Me

Hi! I'm B치rbara Amado, a Rigging Artist in constant development with a strong foundation in programming and a great passion for bringing characters and objects to life through functional and efficient rigs.

I studied Video Game Design and Development, where I specialized in programming and collaborated as a member of the indie studio Silver Tales Studio on the development of A Flower's Dream: The Beginning, a 2D video game. Being part of this project was an incredible experience that taught me to work as part of a team and adapt to a complete production pipeline, understanding the importance of collaboration and organization at every stage of development.

My interest in rigging emerged from combining my technical programming skills with my passion for animation. To deepen my expertise in this area, I completed the Professional Rigging Course at Lightbox Academy, where I learned to create both body and facial rigs for characters and props using Maya, while also exploring Python scripting to automate processes.

Currently, my goal is to continue developing intuitive and efficient rigs that streamline animators' workflows, leveraging my background in scripting and rigging to optimize pipelines and creatively solve technical challenges.

In my free time, I enjoy playing video games of all kinds and exploring new technologies, which constantly inspire me to learn and grow as a professional.
