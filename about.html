About Me

Background

Hi! I'm Bárbara Amado, a Rigging Artist in constant development with a strong foundation in programming and a passion for bringing characters and objects to life through functional and efficient rigs.

I studied Video Game Design and Development, specializing in programming. I collaborated with the indie studio Silver Tales Studio on A Flower's Dream: The Beginning, a 2D game, an experience that taught me how to work in a team and adapt to a full production pipeline.

Rigging experience

My interest in rigging grew from combining programming skills with animation. I completed the Professional Rigging Course at Lightbox Academy, learning body and facial rigs in Maya and applying Python scripting to automate tasks.

I build rigs focused on animator-friendly controls (FK/IK systems, facial blendshapes, deformation helpers) and tools that speed up pipelines.

Current goals

My goal is to keep developing intuitive rigs and scripts that streamline animator workflows, leveraging scripting and rigging knowledge to optimize pipelines and solve technical challenges creatively.

Personal

In my free time I enjoy playing video games and exploring new technologies that inspire me to learn and grow.

Thank you for visiting. If you'd like to know more or collaborate, get in touch.
