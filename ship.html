Ship

The rig of the ship's hull and most of the objects on the deck have been implemented using parent and scale Constraints.

FK Chains

For some objects on the deck and masts, it has been decided to make an FK chain due to their composition and the dependence of the elements that make up the object. In the case of the masts, this system was implemented to ensure that the sail support can rotate while following the movement of the mast itself.

Sails

The sail rig is mainly composed of deformers so that the animator can simulate the behaviour of the sails without the need to apply any type of physics to the ship. For this purpose, a Bend type deformer, a Sine type deformer and a Twist type deformer have been used to control the behaviour of the sails when they are hoisted or when the animator wants to simulate the wind blowing.
