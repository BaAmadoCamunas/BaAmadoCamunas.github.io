Car

Constraints - IK Handles - Set Driven Keys

3D model from MichaelMilesGallie, CGTrader.



Car Body

The rig of the car body has been created by using various parent and scale Constraints and making use of the hierarchy of the bones and controls that compose it in order to achieve obtain specific controls such as the one that controls the car's body, which allow the animator to simulate a more realistic behaviour of the model (effect of inertia and gravity when the car brakes or accelerates).

Windows, Steering Wheel and Wipers

For the windows, steering wheel and wipers, Set Driven Keys have been used to: make the window close and open when the values of an attribute are changed, rotate the wheels according to where the steering wheel is rotated and move the windscreen wipers according to the rotation of the wipers. In addition to this, to ensure that each window has the aforementioned functionality, two auto groups have been created (one for window rotation and one for translation).

Levers and pedals

In the case of the car's levers and pedals, ikHandles have been used to make these elements behave more realistically, moving and rotating from their base.
