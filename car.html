Car

Constraints · IK Handles · Set Driven Keys

See on Vimeo

3D model from MichaelMilesGallie, CGTrader.



This car rig demonstrates the use of parent and scale constraints, IK handles and Set Driven Keys to provide animators with precise control over the vehicle. The rig allows realistic simulation of body movement, steering, pedals and wipers, making it easier to animate acceleration, braking and other interactions with accuracy and fluidity.



Car Body

The car body rig was created using parent and scale constraints, taking advantage of the hierarchical structure of bones and controls.

This setup provides animators with precise control over body movements, enabling realistic responses to acceleration and braking, including inertia and gravity effects.

Windows, Steering Wheel and Wipers

Set Driven Keys were implemented for the windows, steering wheel and wipers to simulate realistic interactions.

Windows open and close based on attribute changes, wheels rotate according to steering wheel movement and wipers move according to the rotation of the controls.

In addition to this, two auto groups were created to ensure independent functionality for rotation and translation of each window.

Levers and pedals

The levers and pedals are rigged using IK Handles, allowing realistic movement and rotation from their base points. This system ensures smooth and intuitive control for the animator when simulating vehicle interactions.
