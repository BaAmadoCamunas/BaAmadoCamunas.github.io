Bow And Arrow

IK Handle · Follow · Set Driven Key

See on Vimeo


This bow and arrow rig has been designed to provide animators with precise control over bow deformation and arrow motion, ensuring realistic animation for cinematic scenes.



Bow

The bow rig consists of two chains of bones, one for the upper and one for the lower section, each controlled by an IK Handle to deform these areas accurately. A Set Driven Key has been implemented so that tightening the bowstring causes the bone chains to deform naturally, simulating the tension generated during bow operation and providing precise control for the animator.

Arrow

The arrow rig has its pivot point moved to the back, enabling more realistic rotation. A Follow attribute allows the arrow to either track or ignore the bow's movement, while an additional attribute governs its self rotation based on an adjustable expression. This setup eliminates the need for the animator to manually calculate the arrow's rotation over time, ensuring smooth and precise control.
