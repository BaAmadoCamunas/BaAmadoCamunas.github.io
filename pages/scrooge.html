Scrooge Rig











Face Rig

Specific controls have been included in the facial rig with the aim of providing greater control to the animator. In addition to this, a FK chain has been created for the lock of hair on the forehead and two Lattice-type deformers have been used: one of them to simulate the movement of the jaw when chewing and another to deform the rest of the hair.



Upper Trunk

For the upper trunk, multiple follows have been created (for the head, shoulders, arms, hands and neck). In addition to this, a Stretch system (for the spine, neck and arms) and a Non Roll system (for the arms) have been made. Likewise, partial joints (in the elbows) and push joints (in the elbows, hand, knuckles and head) have been used to achieve better deformation.



Lower Trunk

In the case of the lower trunk, follows have been created for the feet and leg poles. As in the upper trunk, a Stretch system has been created for the legs and partial joints (for the knees) and push joints (in the hips and knees) have been used to achieve better deformation.
