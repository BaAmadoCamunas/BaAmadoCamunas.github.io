This rig allows the map to roll, stretch and respond to animator input, simulating realistic movement and wind effects for animation projects.



Roll Up the Map

The map has been rigged with Bend and Squash deformers to allow rolling and stretching. A Twist deformer was also applied to provide realistic rotational movement, giving animators precise control over the map's behavior.

Other effects

Additional effects include a Soft Mode to simulate finger pressure and a Sine deformer to emulate wind movement, allowing the animator to create interactive and dynamic map animations.
